Custom Tutoring Problems

I created problems in various subjects for individuals I tutor so they could have material to practice with that would cover the concepts they would be exposed to on exams. The inspiration for this came as a a result of complaints that practice exams set a false precidence for the real exams in certain classes, which would turn out to be different both structurally and sometimes content-wise and would shock the students and result in them performing poorly. To prevent this psychological battle from occuring during hours critical to academic success, I fabricated problems, which students knew would not be on the exam, that covered all the topics they would be exposed to and provided a comparable, if not greater than, degree of difficulty one might find on an exam. This was done in LaTex.
