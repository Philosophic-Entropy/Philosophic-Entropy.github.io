BWXT

  • HFIR Mandrel Stand

    • Attached is a picture of a project I worked on over the summer. The operators wanted a stand to place mandrels on, which go inside of the reactors to maneuver them about the shop in a safe manner. I worked with them to come up with a design, which was extremely cost effective (you know how I feel about government spending and excess waste). In my design plans, which I modeled in a CAD software and simulated the weight that would be present to ensure the structure could support the mandrels, I even accounted for the size of every cut that would be made. I was told that my calculations were exact and there was no space material left. It is made out of 2"x4" tubing and Delren plastic.
